# Details #

  * 12VDC via PSS12-100
  * 19" Rack
  * Low-Power Safety TR Pin Switches
  * Low-Power 50Ohm Dummy load(s) to switch out TX and RX safely to.
  * Elecraft PR6 LNA
  * SMA to RG400 from TX to PA
  * SMA to RG400 to RX from LNA
  * GPSDO
